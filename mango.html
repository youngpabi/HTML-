Mango

High in Vitamin C: Mangoes are a great source of vitamin C, which helps boost the immune system and promotes healthy skin. Digestive Health: Mangoes contain enzymes that aid in digestion and can help relieve constipation. Antioxidants: The high levels of antioxidants in mangoes help protect against various diseases and may reduce the risk of certain cancers. Eye Health: Mangoes are rich in vitamin A, which is essential for maintaining good vision and overall eye health. Skin Health: The vitamins and antioxidants in mangoes can help promote healthy skin and may reduce the signs of aging. In addition to these health benefits, mangoes are versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways, including in smoothies, salads, salsas, and desserts. Overall, mangoes are a delicious and nutritious fruit that can be a great addition to a balanced diet.
